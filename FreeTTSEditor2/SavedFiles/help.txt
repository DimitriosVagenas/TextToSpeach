Welcome to Text2Speech Gus Edition 2020!

In this application you can turn text to audio. 
Use file tab to "open", "save" or create a "new" txt file.

From the "Edit Toggle" option you can enable or disable the
edit text option.

When you load a text you can hear its contents by pressing the 
"Speak" button or part of its contents by selecting the 
"Selected" option.

You can hear the text in normal mode or coded in atBash, Rot13 
and reversed encoding.

Finally you can edit the voice reading the text in the "Edit"
tab and change the pitch, the volume and the words per minute.

HAVE FUN!
